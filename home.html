How to Book a Cab Online on Cabzo?

Cab Booking | A to Z of Booking Online Cab Tickets
  • Enter the Source and Destination locations in the “From” and “To” tab respectively.
  • Select a Cab of your choice from the list of cabes that are displayed.
  • Select a boarding, and dropping points and hit “Proceed to Book” for final cab booking.
  • Fill in the “Passenger Information” and “Contact Information”.
  • You can even choose to insure your cab booking so that you will be covered in case of an accident or loss of luggage and then click “Proceed to Pay”.
  • Enter the offer code (if applicable), select your mode of payment and complete the payment procedure for cab tickets online.
  • Once this is done, you will receive an M-ticket on your mobile and an e-ticket on the email ID that was entered under “Contact Information.”
